Simple IG Analyser is committed to protecting your digital privacy. This policy outlines our data practices when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your Instagram password, personal messages, or private photos. The extension operates by reading publicly available data from your own profile while you are logged in.

2. Local Storage Only

Lists of your followers and following are stored exclusively within your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data is used only to identify non-followers and is never uploaded to any external server or database.

3. Authentication Data

To provide Pro features, your Instagram username is used locally to validate your unique Pro Key. We do not track your usage history.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties. Your data remains strictly on your personal device.
